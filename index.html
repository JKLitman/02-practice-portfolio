About Me

I am a graduate from the University of Minnesota, Twin Cities, where I earned my Bachelor of Arts Degree in English. I have worked for many years in the education field, mostly with young chlidren. I currently am back in school, taking a programming bootcamp offered through the UofM and plan on using the information learned in that course to transition into a new career field in that respective industry. My personal life includes many hobbies, such as painting, writing, frisbee golfing. board games, and anything else I find interest in. I have two cats that attempt to fill the hollow void in my life.
